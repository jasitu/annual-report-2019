Your support makes what we do possible. Thank you for your generosity.

Because of individuals and institutions who made generous donations in Fiscal Year 2019 (from July 1, 2018 to June 30, 2019), Perkins was able to empower and educate students while planning for future success. The impact of your financial support could be as simple as a braille algebra textbook for a student that loves math or as complex as a teacher training in India to better serve students who are blind or visually impaired with multiple disabilities. Thank you from the entire Perkins community.
